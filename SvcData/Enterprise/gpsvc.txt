# Group Policy Client
Applies group policy settings for this computer and its users.

Group policies are OS commands, policies and settings configured by the administrators of the domain or local computer. One way to configure policies is through Group Policy MMC snap-in.

Policies are distributed throughout the domain and applied to each participating PC with the help of this component. PCs

https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/hh125927%28v=ws.10%29.aspx#BKMK_gpsvc

DISABLE: Group Policies will not be applied. Since even local operation depends on group policies these days, probably leave running.
