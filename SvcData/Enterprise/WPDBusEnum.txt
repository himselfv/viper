# Portable Device Enumerator Service
> Enforces Group Policy settings for syncing removable mass-storage devices.
> Enables applications such as Windows Media Player and Image Import Wizard to transfer and synchronize content by using removable mass-storage devices.
> Enables you to specify which portable storage devices are allowed to be connected to systems that are managed with Group Policy.
